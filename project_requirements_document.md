# Ultimate Dominion - Project Requirements Document

## What is Ultimate Dominion?

Ultimate Dominion is a text-based game where many players can play together online. Think of old-school text adventures, but modernized with blockchain technology. Instead of just reading and typing commands, players can own their items for real using blockchain, just like owning digital art or collectibles.

Our goal is simple: we want to create a game that feels like the classic text adventures many of us grew up with, but with modern features that let players truly own their in-game stuff and trade with each other fairly.

## How Will Players Experience the Game?

When someone first joins Ultimate Dominion, they'll need a digital wallet - think of it like a special account that holds their game items. After connecting their wallet, they'll create their character. They can choose what type of hero they want to be, set their character's strengths and skills, and pick a name.

New players will go through a friendly tutorial that shows them the basics - how to move around, fight monsters, collect items, and trade with others. It's like learning the rules of a new board game, but you can go at your own pace.

Once players know the basics, they can start their adventure. They'll explore different areas of the game world, fight monsters, find treasure, and meet other players. They can join groups called guilds, trade items in the marketplace, and even fight other players if they want to.

## How Does the Game Work Behind the Scenes?

We're building Ultimate Dominion with some powerful tools. The game's front end - what players see and interact with - uses React, which helps us make smooth, responsive interfaces. We're using Chakra UI to make everything look good and work well on different screen sizes.

The game's brain lives on the blockchain using something called the MUD engine. Think of MUD as a special game engine that lets us store game data securely and make sure everything is fair. When players trade items or fight monsters, MUD helps make sure everything happens correctly and fairly.

We also have a regular server (using Node.js) that handles things that don't need to be on the blockchain, like chat messages or temporary game data. This helps keep the game fast and cheap to play, since not everything needs to be stored on the blockchain.

## What Can Players Do in the Game?

The heart of Ultimate Dominion is about growing your character and exploring the world. Players can:

- Create unique characters with different skills and abilities
- Fight monsters and other players in turn-based battles
- Collect items, weapons, and resources
- Trade with other players in a secure marketplace
- Join guilds and make friends
- Complete quests and missions
- Own territory and build their reputation

We're starting with the basics first - making sure players can create characters, fight, trade, and socialize. Later, we'll add more advanced features like guild wars, player housing, and complex crafting systems.

## What Makes the Game Special?

Unlike traditional online games where the company owns everything, Ultimate Dominion lets players truly own their items through blockchain technology. If you find a rare sword or earn special armor, it's really yours - you can keep it, sell it, or trade it as you wish.

The game's economy is also special because it's controlled by players, not us. Prices for items are set by what players are willing to pay, just like in a real marketplace. This makes the game feel more alive and gives players more control over their gaming experience.

## What Challenges Are We Preparing For?

Building a game like this comes with some interesting challenges. We need to make sure:

- The game runs smoothly even when lots of players are online
- Everything is secure and fair
- Players don't have to wait too long for blockchain transactions
- New players can easily learn how to use digital wallets
- The game economy stays balanced and fun
- Players have a good time whether they play a little or a lot

We're handling these challenges by:

- Testing everything thoroughly before adding new features
- Having security experts check our code
- Listening to player feedback
- Rolling out new features gradually
- Keeping a close eye on the game's economy
- Creating clear guides and help resources
- Making the game run as fast as possible

## Looking Ahead

While we're starting with core features like character creation, basic combat, and trading, we have big plans for the future. We want to add things like player housing, more complex guild features, and maybe even a mobile version of the game. But we're taking it one step at a time to make sure everything works well and is fun to play.

Our main focus is on making a game that's fun, fair, and gives players real ownership of their gaming experience. We believe that by combining classic game elements with modern blockchain technology, we can create something special that players will enjoy for years to come.
