You wake in a cave with no memory and no name. Everything after that is yours.

Ultimate Dominion is a persistent on-chain MUD — a world that runs forever, where every piece of gold, every weapon, every scar belongs to you. Not to a server. Not to us. You can prove it.

## What's Live

The **Dark Cave** is open. A 10x10 grid of torchlit tunnels, danger zones, and monsters that hit back. Three races, nine advanced classes, turn-based combat that rewards strategy over speed.

- **PvE**: Fight your way through the Dark Cave. Every monster has unique drops.
- **PvP**: Enter danger zones at your own risk. Gold on the line, no takebacks.
- **Marketplace**: Trade items with other players. Real economy, real stakes.
- **Permanent progression**: Your character exists on-chain. Level up, gear up, build your legacy.

## No Downloads. No Crypto Jargon.

Open your browser. Create a character. The blockchain is invisible — you'll never see a wallet prompt or gas fee. We handle all of that so you can focus on surviving.

## This Is Just the Beginning

The Dark Cave is the first zone. More are coming. More monsters, more lore, more reasons to come back tomorrow and the day after that.

This is not a game you finish. It's a world that becomes part of you.
